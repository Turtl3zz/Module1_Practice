About Sea Turtles

Fun Facts about the Wanderers of the Sea

Sea Turtles have lived over 100 million years, outlasting the Dinosuars. Sea Turtles cant retract into their shells, they are actually apart of their shells. It fuses with their spine. Sea Turtles come to shore at night and lay their eggs. The temperatures of the sand help determine the sex of the baby turtles, the warmer the sand means its likely female, the cooler the sand is likely males. Sadly Sea Turtles are endangered so it is highly asked to not interact with them.
